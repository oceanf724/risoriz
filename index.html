Risograph printing is a technique best described as “digital screen printing”. Which is an accurate description because the process is very similar to screen screen printing, but with the convience of an office printer. It’s known best for its vivid colors and unique printed textures. And it serves as an easy and popular way to print posters, zines, prints, and so much more.
